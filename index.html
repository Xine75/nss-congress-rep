Rep. Jim Cooper

Cooper is the representative for Tennessee's 5th Congressional District.

Party Affiliation: Democrat

Cooper serves on the following committees:

  1. House Committee on Armed Services
  2. House Budget Committee
  3. House Committee on Oversight and Reform.

Legislation he has enacted:

  • HR 482: Water Heater Rating Improvement Act
  • HR 928 Inspector General Reform Act of 2008
  • HJ 297 To Designate 1994 as "The Year of Gospel Music"
  • HR 5667 To Amend the Water Resources Development Act of 1974
  • HR 971 Telephone Operator Consumer Services Improvement Act of 1990
